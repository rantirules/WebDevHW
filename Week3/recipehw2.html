    HOW TO COOK RISOTTO

    Ingredients

    • Sunflower Oil
    • Arborio Rice
    • Chicken Stoke Cube
    • Cooked Chicken
    • White wine
    • Manda Chilli sauce or any hot chilli sauce
    • Mushrooms
    • Peppers
      •

    Instructions

    1. Add a drizzle of sunflower oil and sliced medium red onion to the pot.
    2. Put the gas on and leave to sizzle
    3. Add 3 cups of risotto rice to the pot, then add 100g of butter.
    4. Add half a mini bottle of white wine.
    5. In a separate bowl add 2 chicken stock cubes and 3.5 cups of boiling water.
    6. Leave to dissolve.
    7. Slowly add this mixture to the pot.
    8. After you have emptied Â¾ of the bowl, add salt.
    9. Add a spoonful of manda chilli sauce to the pot.
    10. Add a handful of sliced mushrooms to the pot.
    11. Keep on adding chicken stock mixture, as it evaporates.
    12. Leave to simmer for 2 minutes.
    13. Once the rice has started to go brown, sprinkle the pre-soaked torn chicken.
    14. Stir, stir, stir
    15. Leave to simmer
    16. Serve!
